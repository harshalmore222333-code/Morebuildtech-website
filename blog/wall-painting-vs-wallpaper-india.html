Wall Painting vs Wallpaper India

July 09, 202610 min readMoreBuildtech Expert TeamMumbai

When it comes to decorating your home, two popular options are wall painting vs wallpaper India. In this article, we will explore the pros and cons of each option to help you decide which one is best for your Indian home. As a resident of Mumbai, Maharashtra, you know how important it is to have a beautiful and durable interior design that can withstand the city's humid climate.

Introduction to Wall Painting

Wall painting is a traditional and cost-effective way to decorate your walls. The cost of wall painting in India can range from ₹10 to ₹50 per square foot, depending on the type of paint and the location. In Mumbai, the cost of wall painting can be higher due to the city's high demand for interior design services.

Types of Wall Paints

There are several types of wall paints available in the market, including emulsion paint, enamel paint, and distemper paint. Emulsion paint is a popular choice for Indian homes due to its water-resistant properties and affordability.

Introduction to Wallpaper

Wallpaper is a modern and stylish way to decorate your walls. The cost of wallpaper in India can range from ₹50 to ₹500 per roll, depending on the design, quality, and brand. In Maharashtra, the cost of wallpaper can be higher due to the state's high demand for luxury interior design products.

Types of Wallpapers

There are several types of wallpapers available in the market, including vinyl wallpaper, paper wallpaper, and non-woven wallpaper. Vinyl wallpaper is a popular choice for Indian homes due to its water-resistant properties and ease of installation.

Wall Painting vs Wallpaper: Durability

When it comes to durability, wall painting is a better option than wallpaper. Wall painting can last for up to 5 years without fading or peeling, while wallpaper can last for up to 3 years. However, the durability of wallpaper depends on the quality of the product and the installation process.

Wall Painting vs Wallpaper: Maintenance

When it comes to maintenance, wall painting is a better option than wallpaper. Wall painting requires minimal maintenance, while wallpaper requires regular cleaning and maintenance to prevent dust and dirt from accumulating.

Wall Painting vs Wallpaper: Design Options

When it comes to design options, wallpaper is a better option than wall painting. Wallpaper comes in a wide range of designs, patterns, and colors, while wall painting is limited to solid colors and textures.

Wall Painting vs Wallpaper: Cost-Effectiveness

When it comes to cost-effectiveness, wall painting is a better option than wallpaper. The cost of wall painting is significantly lower than the cost of wallpaper, making it a more affordable option for Indian homeowners.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the choice between wall painting vs wallpaper India depends on your personal preferences, budget, and lifestyle. If you are looking for a cost-effective and durable option, wall painting may be the better choice. However, if you are looking for a stylish and modern option, wallpaper may be the better choice.
