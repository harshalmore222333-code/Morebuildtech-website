Vastu Shastra Home Construction Tips

June 21, 202610 min readMoreBuildtech Expert TeamMumbai

Vastu Shastra home construction is an essential aspect of building a new home in India, as it helps in creating a harmonious and balanced living space. The concept of Vastu Shastra is based on the ancient Indian science of architecture, which takes into account the five elements of nature - earth, water, fire, air, and space. By incorporating Vastu Shastra principles in home construction, homeowners can ensure a peaceful and prosperous living environment.

Vastu Shastra Principles for Home Construction

The main objective of Vastu Shastra is to create a balance between the physical and metaphysical elements of a building. This is achieved by carefully planning the layout, design, and construction of the home. Some of the key Vastu Shastra principles for home construction include the selection of a suitable plot, orientation of the building, and placement of rooms and doors.

Selection of a Suitable Plot

The selection of a suitable plot is a crucial aspect of Vastu Shastra home construction. The plot should be square or rectangular in shape, with a length that is 1.5 times the width. The plot should also be free from any obstacles, such as trees, wells, or other buildings.

Vastu Shastra Tips for Home Interior Design

Vastu Shastra also plays a significant role in home interior design. The placement of furniture, color scheme, and lighting can all impact the energy and ambiance of a room. For example, the bedroom should be placed in the southwest direction, while the kitchen should be placed in the southeast direction.

Importance of Color Scheme

The color scheme of a room can also impact the energy and ambiance. Different colors are associated with different emotions and energies, and should be chosen carefully. For example, the color blue is associated with calmness and tranquility, while the color red is associated with energy and passion.

Common Mistakes to Avoid in Vastu Shastra Home Construction

There are several common mistakes to avoid in Vastu Shastra home construction. These include the placement of a bedroom under a staircase, the construction of a kitchen in the northwest direction, and the use of a color scheme that is not in harmony with the energy of the room. By avoiding these mistakes, homeowners can ensure that their home is Vastu Shastra compliant and enjoys a positive and harmonious energy.
