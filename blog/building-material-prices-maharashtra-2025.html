Building Material Prices in Maharashtra

June 06, 202610 min readMoreBuildtech Expert TeamMumbai

The construction industry in Maharashtra, especially in cities like Mumbai, is booming. Understanding the current building material prices Maharashtra is crucial for planning and executing construction projects. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive guide to the latest building material prices in Maharashtra 2025.

Introduction to Building Material Prices in Maharashtra

The cost of building materials in Maharashtra can vary depending on the location, quality, and quantity. As of 2025, the prices of building materials in Maharashtra are as follows: cement costs around INR 400-500 per bag, steel costs around INR 60-80 per kg, and sand costs around INR 40-60 per cubic foot.

Cement Prices in Maharashtra 2025

Cement Types and Prices

There are different types of cement available in Maharashtra, including PPC, OPC, and PSC. The prices of these cements vary, with PPC costing around INR 400-450 per bag, OPC costing around INR 450-500 per bag, and PSC costing around INR 500-550 per bag.

Steel Prices in Maharashtra 2025

Steel Types and Prices

There are different types of steel available in Maharashtra, including mild steel, torsion steel, and high-strength steel. The prices of these steels vary, with mild steel costing around INR 50-60 per kg, torsion steel costing around INR 60-70 per kg, and high-strength steel costing around INR 70-80 per kg.

Sand and Aggregate Prices in Maharashtra 2025

The prices of sand and aggregate in Maharashtra can vary depending on the location and quality. As of 2025, the prices of sand and aggregate in Maharashtra are as follows: sand costs around INR 40-60 per cubic foot, and aggregate costs around INR 30-50 per cubic foot.

Brick and Block Prices in Maharashtra 2025

Brick Types and Prices

There are different types of bricks available in Maharashtra, including clay bricks, fly ash bricks, and concrete bricks. The prices of these bricks vary, with clay bricks costing around INR 5-7 per piece, fly ash bricks costing around INR 4-6 per piece, and concrete bricks costing around INR 6-8 per piece.

Wood and Plywood Prices in Maharashtra 2025

The prices of wood and plywood in Maharashtra can vary depending on the quality and quantity. As of 2025, the prices of wood and plywood in Maharashtra are as follows: wood costs around INR 50-100 per cubic foot, and plywood costs around INR 40-80 per sheet.

Construction Labor Costs in Maharashtra 2025

The construction labor costs in Maharashtra can vary depending on the location, skill level, and experience. As of 2025, the construction labor costs in Maharashtra are as follows: unskilled labor costs around INR 500-700 per day, semi-skilled labor costs around INR 700-1000 per day, and skilled labor costs around INR 1000-1500 per day.
