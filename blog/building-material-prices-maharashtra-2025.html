Building Material Prices in Maharashtra

July 04, 202610 min readMoreBuildtech Expert TeamMumbai

The building material prices in Maharashtra have been fluctuating over the past few years, affecting the construction industry in Mumbai and other parts of the state. As of 2025, the prices of essential building materials such as cement, steel, and sand have increased significantly, impacting the overall cost of construction projects.

Current Trends in Building Material Prices

The construction industry in Maharashtra is experiencing a surge in demand for building materials, leading to higher prices. The cost of cement, for example, has risen to around INR 400-500 per bag, while steel prices have increased to INR 60,000-70,000 per ton.

Cement Prices in Maharashtra 2025

Factors Affecting Cement Prices

The price of cement in Maharashtra is influenced by various factors, including production costs, transportation, and government taxes. The current price of cement in Mumbai is around INR 450-500 per bag, depending on the brand and quality.

Steel Prices in Maharashtra 2025

The cost of steel in Maharashtra has increased significantly due to the rising demand from the construction and infrastructure sectors. The current price of steel in Mumbai is around INR 65,000-75,000 per ton, depending on the type and quality of the material.

Sand and Aggregate Prices in Maharashtra

Impact of Government Regulations

The prices of sand and aggregate in Maharashtra have been affected by government regulations aimed at curbing illegal mining activities. The current price of sand in Mumbai is around INR 30,000-40,000 per brass, depending on the quality and source.

Bricks and Concrete Blocks Prices

The cost of bricks and concrete blocks in Maharashtra has increased due to the rising demand for these materials in the construction industry. The current price of bricks in Mumbai is around INR 5,000-6,000 per thousand, depending on the quality and type.

Timber and Wood Prices in Maharashtra

The prices of timber and wood in Maharashtra have been affected by the rising demand from the construction and furniture industries. The current price of timber in Mumbai is around INR 1,500-2,500 per cubic foot, depending on the type and quality of the material.

Overall, the building material prices in Maharashtra are expected to continue rising in the coming years, driven by the growing demand from the construction industry. However, with careful planning and budgeting, it is possible to manage the costs and ensure a successful construction project.
