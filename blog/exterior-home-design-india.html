Exterior Home Design India: Ideas & Trends

July 14, 202610 min readMoreBuildtech Expert TeamMumbai

When it comes to exterior home design India, there are numerous options to consider, from modern to traditional and everything in between. As a homeowner in Mumbai or Maharashtra, you want your home's exterior to reflect your personal style and complement the surrounding architecture. In this article, we will explore the latest trends and ideas in exterior home design India, including paint colors, elevation designs, and more.

Exterior Paint Colors for Indian Homes

Choosing the right exterior paint colors for your Indian home can be a daunting task, but with a little guidance, you can make an informed decision. Consider the climate, surroundings, and style of your home when selecting a color scheme. In Mumbai, for example, it's essential to choose colors that can withstand the harsh sun and heavy rainfall.

Popular Exterior Paint Colors in India

  • Soft pastels
  • Earth tones
  • Bold brights

Home Elevation Design Ideas for Indian Homes

A well-designed home elevation can make a significant impact on your home's exterior appearance. Consider factors like natural light, ventilation, and architectural style when designing your home's elevation. In Maharashtra, many homeowners opt for traditional elevation designs that incorporate local materials and craftsmanship.

Modern Home Elevation Design Trends

Some popular modern home elevation design trends in India include:

  • Minimalist facades
  • Large windows and doors
  • Sustainable materials

Exterior Home Design Trends in India

Stay up-to-date with the latest exterior home design trends in India, from modern to traditional and everything in between. Some popular trends include:

  • Outdoor living spaces
  • Green roofs and walls
  • Energy-efficient features

Cost of Exterior Home Design in India

The cost of exterior home design in India can vary greatly depending on factors like location, materials, and design complexity. On average, homeowners in Mumbai can expect to pay between ₹50,000 to ₹5 lakhs for exterior painting services, while home elevation design services can cost between ₹1 lakh to ₹10 lakhs or more.
