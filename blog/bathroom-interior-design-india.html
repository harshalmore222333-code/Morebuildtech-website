Bathroom Interior Design India

July 24, 202610 min readMoreBuildtech Expert TeamMumbai

When it comes to bathroom interior design India, there are numerous options to choose from, especially in a city like Mumbai, where space is a constraint. At MoreBuildtech, we understand the importance of creating a functional and luxurious bathroom space that reflects your personal style. In this blog post, we will explore the latest trends and ideas in bathroom interior design India, to help you create your dream bathroom.

Modern Bathroom Fixtures and Fittings

In recent years, there has been a significant shift towards modern bathroom fixtures and fittings in Indian homes. These include wall-mounted toilets, rain showerheads, and sleek sink basins. Not only do these fixtures add a touch of luxury to your bathroom, but they also help to create a sense of spaciousness.

Cost of Modern Bathroom Fixtures

The cost of modern bathroom fixtures can vary greatly, depending on the brand and quality. On average, a wall-mounted toilet can cost anywhere from ₹20,000 to ₹50,000, while a rain showerhead can cost between ₹5,000 to ₹15,000.

Bathroom Storage Solutions

A well-designed bathroom storage system is essential for maintaining a clutter-free and organized space. This can include custom-made cabinets, shelving units, and drawers. At MoreBuildtech, we offer a range of bathroom storage solutions that are both functional and stylish.

Luxury Bathroom Materials and Finishes

Luxury bathroom materials and finishes can add a touch of sophistication to your bathroom space. Some popular options include marble, granite, and quartz. These materials are not only durable but also easy to maintain, making them a great choice for Indian homeowners.

Cost of Luxury Bathroom Materials

The cost of luxury bathroom materials can vary greatly, depending on the quality and quantity. On average, marble flooring can cost anywhere from ₹200 to ₹500 per square foot, while granite countertops can cost between ₹300 to ₹700 per square foot.

Bathroom Lighting Ideas

Proper lighting is essential for creating a warm and inviting bathroom space. Some popular bathroom lighting ideas include LED lights, sconces, and recessed lighting. At MoreBuildtech, we offer a range of bathroom lighting solutions that are both energy-efficient and stylish.

Bathroom Renovation Ideas

A bathroom renovation can be a daunting task, especially in a city like Mumbai, where space is a constraint. However, with the right planning and expertise, it is possible to create a luxurious and functional bathroom space. Some popular bathroom renovation ideas include creating a wet area, installing a freestanding bathtub, and using glass partitions.

Cost of Bathroom Renovation

The cost of a bathroom renovation can vary greatly, depending on the scope of work and materials used. On average, a basic bathroom renovation can cost anywhere from ₹1.5 lakhs to ₹3 lakhs, while a luxury bathroom renovation can cost between ₹5 lakhs to ₹10 lakhs.

Bathroom Interior Design Trends
